A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Biography

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was a renowned scientist, politician, and statesman who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, a small town in Tamil Nadu, India.Kalam was a humble and down-to-earth person who inspired many with his leadership and vision for a better India. He was a prolific writer and authored several books on science, education, and spirituality. Kalam believed in empowering the youth and frequently interacted with students to encourage them to pursue careers in science and technology. Kalam passed away on July 27, 2015, while delivering a lecture at the Indian Institute of Management Shillong. He will always be remembered as a role model and inspiration to millions around the world.
